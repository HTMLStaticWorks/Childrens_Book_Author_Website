Common Questions

Do you travel internationally?

Yes! While I am based in Storyville, I love traveling to meet readers abroad. Travel and accommodation fees apply for locations outside a 50-mile radius.

What age groups do you present to?

I have tailored sessions for Early Years (Pre-K) all the way up to Middle School (Grades 6-8). My keynote speeches are designed for adults/educators.

Are books available for pre-order?

Absolutely. We provide a pre-order form for schools to distribute to parents two weeks before the visit to ensure every child gets their signed copy.

Can we record the session?

Virtual visits may be recorded for internal school use only. Recording of physical assemblies is permitted with prior written consent.
