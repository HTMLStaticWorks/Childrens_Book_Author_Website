What's the Story?

Leo was a quiet boy who loved his telescope. But one Tuesday, the stars didn't just twinkle—they waved. Join Leo as he climbs a ladder of moonlight to visit the constellation of the Great Bear and helps find a lost shooting star.

Designed to be read before bedtime, this story uses rhythmic prose and soothing nighttime imagery to calm curious minds and prepare them for their own starry dreams.
